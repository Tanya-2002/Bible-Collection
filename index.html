GO FURTHER

Go Further with Filament

Suddenly, you can go so much further with your Bible reading and study without the bulk of a traditional study Bible.

Unbounded by size, weight, or format, Bibles equipped with the Filament Bible app bring you spot-on study notes, articles, reflections, videos, interactive maps, and even worship music—all curated for the page you're reading—when you scan the page number with your phone or tablet.

For centuries, one had to choose between small, easy-to-carry Bibles with few notes or a door-stop-sized Bible with voluminous on-page helps. NOW, you just need to select from a large variety of Bibles equipped with Filament to get it all.

PRINT + DIGITAL = UNSURPASSED UNDERSTANDING

The Filament Bible Collection is available in a variety of colors, sizes, text fonts, and styles. All have been beautifully constructed with lay-flat, Smyth-Sewn binding; stunning cover designs; and access to the revolutionary Filament Bible app.

Editions in the new journaling collection also have thick, opaque, cream-colored journaling paper, and every other page is blank, allowing space for you to create and interact through art, Bible journaling, note-taking, written prayers, and more.

Thinline Reference editions are available in full size and large print.These thin, easy-to-carry Bibles also include thousands of cross- references.

Thinline Center-Column Reference editions have a traditional two column style with a cross-reference system in the center as well as other reference features including the Tyndale Hebrew and Greek word-study system.

Personal Size Giant Print editions offer an easy to read font in a portable size.

Super Giant Print editions have the largestfont size in the line yet still are easy to carry with you.

Giant Print bridges the gap between the Personal Size Giant Print and the Super Giant Print for an enjoyable reading experience. Also available in Premium Value editions.

Compact Giant Print brings you the best of portability and readability with a larger font in a smaller sized Bible.

Compact editions are the smallest Bibles in the collection. They are a perfect size for people on-the-go, as they can easily be slipped into a bag or backpack. Some even come with zipper covers. Also available in Premium Value editions.

Premium Value Thinline editions are affordable Bibles with beautiful covers. They are available in full size and large print.

Journaling editions are individual volumes that contain one or more books of the New Testament. Every other page is blank, allowing space for art, journaling, and note- taking. Also available in box sets.

Wide Margin Filament Bibles are single column and include 2.25" lightly ruled margins, making this Bible great for note-taking, journaling, and more.

DaySpring Signature Collection is a limited series of stunning cover designs made in partnership with the artists at DaySpring. They are available in wide margin, thinline reference, thinline reference large print, personal size giant print, and super giant print

Single Column deluxe editions have stunning cover designs and extra-thick Bible paper.

FILAMENT APP FEATURES

Illuminate classic print Bibles with Filament's 21st century technology

STUDY

The Study window contains everything you would expect to find in an excellent study Bible: book introductions, study notes, in-depth articles on key topics, and profiles of key people from the Bible.

REFLECT

The Reflect window is less about in- depth study and more about connecting the ideas of the Bible to your everyday lite. Here you'll find devotional articles and thoughts that are curated from some of Tyndale's favorite resources

SEE

The See window is where you'll find all things visual. Interactive maps and visuals allow you to zoom in or out and tap key locations to learn more and see photos of the sites. Videos that are relevant to the page you are reading are provided by The Bible Project
